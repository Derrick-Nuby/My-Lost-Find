Embrace What's Found - MyLostFind.Com

Welcome to MyLostFind.Com, your dedicated platform for finding lost documents and treasured items in Rwanda. Losing important documents can be stressful and time-consuming, but we're here to make the recovery process a breeze. Whether it's a misplaced ID card, a missing passport, or other valuable belongings, our community-driven website connects you with those who have found items and are eager to reunite them with their rightful owners. Submit your lost item details and let our advanced system work its magic, while also being a hero by reporting found items. Together, let's bring peace of mind back into your life!

Step 1: Report Your Lost Item

  • Easily create an account or log in.
  • Provide details about the lost item: type and document number.
  • Our system generates a unique ticket for your lost item.

Step 2: Discover a Found Item

  • Community members report found items.
  • Our advanced system matches lost and found items.
  • You receive real-time notifications when a match is found.

Step 3: Reclaim Your Item

  • Review details of the found item.
  • Choose a convenient delivery location on Google Maps.
  • Make a secure online payment for retrieval.
  • Enjoy a seamless item return process.
